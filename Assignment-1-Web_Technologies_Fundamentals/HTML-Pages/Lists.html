Correct Pushups Forms

Here are the correct forms for doing pushups

  1. Warm up before starting
  2. Keep your body straight
  3. Lower your body until chest touches the ground
  4. Be in Plank postion
  5. Engage your core muscles
  6. Engage your Glutes
  7. Slowly go down chest touching the ground
  8. Inhaling
  9. Push back up to the starting position
  10. Exhaling

    11. Mistakes to Avoid

    • Slouching your shoulders
    • Not keeping your body straight
    • Not engaing your core
    • Not enaging glutes, lead to hip sagging
    • Using momentum
    • Not breathing properly
